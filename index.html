about us

we are a zero waste market and vegetarian coffee shop. we sell organic dried foods, fruit and vegetables and zero waste cleaning as well as personal hygiene products. join us on our journey to become more sustainable and eco-friendly.

our food and drink

we have a wide range of vegan and vegetarian breakfasts, lunches, snacks and desserts. we source locally, responsibly and organically where possible and we make a mean coffee - with every milk option you can think of!

zero waste market

we have one of the largest plastic free, self serve dried goods markets around and we recently introdcued fresh organic fruits and vegetables. our mission is to inspire and educate as many people as possible to lead a plastic free, meat free, healthy lifestyle.
